About Us

Fostering the Entrepreneurial Drive

Welcome to the Institution's Innovation Council (IIC) cum Entrepreneurship Cell at BIT Sindri. Established to systematically foster the culture of Innovation and startup ecosystem within the institute, we guide mindsets from ideation to incubation.


Our primary focus is to create a vibrant local ecosystem, identify student scouting mechanisms, and establish a functional ecosystem for searching and nurturing concepts into viable industrial platforms.
